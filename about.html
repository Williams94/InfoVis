This information visualisation shows the Human Development Index of each country around the world, if they have a HDI. The Human Development Index is used to show how developed a country is, taking into account; Life Expectancy, Expected School Years, Actual School Years and Gross National Income (PPP $). Unlike other indexes the HDI does not just rely on economic factors, it takes into account other factors listed above that are vital for a developed country.
